Memory Unblocker

Tool: #cognitive, #unblocker

A simple prayer that will unblock most memories that need healing.

The Memory Unblocker is a type of Intercessory and Personal Prayer to unblock a person’s recollection and retelling of a story, or at least the traumatic part of the story. The following diagram shows how to get a story and retell it:

Painful and significant stories in a person’s life are either revealed by God, or recalled from the person’s memory. Blockages to accessing that story are either internal to the person (e.g. a vow to not remember), or external to the person (e.g. a curse to not hear from God, or a covenant made with another person to not remember).

Even if the person has the story, they may have made an inner vow to never tell anyone what happened, or be put under a curse if they were to tell an abuser’s secret. So, those output blockages need to be removed before the person can tell their story.

The Memory Unblocker prayer deals with internal and external blockages, as well as the input and output stages of getting a story and retelling it in one short prayer. It says,

You as the intercessor can then pray, “God, please break those blockages to let <Person> tell their story.” The Memory Unblocker works for most cases of blocked memories. If the person is still blocked, you will have to use higher-end spiritual warfare tools to unblock more heavily defended stories, or heal other stories before coming back to this one.

Take-aways

Stories come from divine revelation or human recollection

Divine revelation requires unblocked hearing, and human recollection requires an unblocked memory.

Blockages can be internal or external to the person

An inner vow is internal to the person, while someone else’s curse to never tell is external to the person.

Blockages can be at the input stage, or the output stage

A person may have problems recalling a story (input), and / or retelling a story (output). You need to unblock both stages to get the story.

The Memory Unblocker works for simple cases

Harder cases with more heavily defended stories will require stronger spiritual warfare tools to unblock the memory.

Further Reading

The Memory Unblocker is the main unblocker tool of the Story Method, although it can be used in other healing methods like the Whiteboard Method. It is a specialized form of the Intercessory and Personal Prayers, so the theological reasons why the Memory Unblocker works can be found in that article.
